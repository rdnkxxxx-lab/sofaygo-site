Story

The First
Recognition

Breaking through with his debut project, SoFaygo captured the attention of the industry with his unique blend of trap melodies and emotional storytelling. His co-sign by Travis Scott's Cactus Jack label marked a defining moment in his career.


His raw vulnerability and relentless artistry have earned him a devoted fanbase that continues to grow with each release — cementing his place as one of Atlanta's most promising voices.

Signed to Travis Scott's Cactus Jack Records, SoFaygo joined a roster defined by creative boundary-pushing. The label gave him the platform and freedom to evolve his craft and connect with a global audience on his own terms.


Under Cactus Jack, SoFaygo has collaborated with some of the biggest names in hip-hop while maintaining the raw authenticity that first made him stand out from the crowd.

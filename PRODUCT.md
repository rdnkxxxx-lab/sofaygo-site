# SoFaygo — Official Fan Site

## Users
Hip-hop listeners aged 16–28, primarily SoFaygo fans and Cactus Jack label followers. They discover the site through social links or searches. Mood: curious, culturally tuned-in, visually literate. They expect music, visuals, artist lore — not corporate polish.

## Product Purpose
An immersive artist showcase for SoFaygo (Andre Dontrel Burt Jr.), rapper/singer from Atlanta signed to Travis Scott's Cactus Jack Records. The site communicates his identity, discography, and music streaming links. Design IS the product — the atmosphere and feel are as important as the content.

## Brand Voice
Dark, cinematic, restrained. Southern trap with melodic softness. Not aggressive-maximalist — quiet intensity. Monochromatic with an occasional flash of color (pink/white accent).

## Tone
Confident, sparse, slightly enigmatic. No hype-speak. The music does the talking; the site sets the stage.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS white-mode landing pages
- Flashy hip-hop sites with neon gradients everywhere
- Overcrowded layouts
- Generic editorial magazine aesthetics (Cormorant + ruled columns)

## Existing commitments (identity-preservation)
- Bebas Neue for display type — core brand voice
- Modak for logo only
- Inter for body (already shipped — preserve, optimize usage)
- Dark base: #080808
- Theme switcher: "Go+" (dark/white) ↔ "Pink Heartz" (#FF1493)
- Intro overlay animation, scroll reveal, gooey text morph, Spotify player
- Sections: Nav → Hero → Video → Recognition → Gooey Band → Songs → Angelic 7 → Cactus Jack → Footer

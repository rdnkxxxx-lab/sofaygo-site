His Sound

Melodic vocals draped over dark 808s. Emotional rawness that cuts through. SoFaygo bends Atlanta trap into something deeply personal — vulnerability as aggression, softness as a weapon. Signed to Travis Scott's Cactus Jack, he carries that cinematic weight in every bar.

The Timeline
  • 2019
    Drops War — raw early tape, building his voice out of Atlanta streets
  • 2020
    Releases Angelic 7 then After Me — his most acclaimed independent run
  • 2021
    Gains viral momentum. Travis Scott and Cactus Jack take notice
  • 2022
    Signs to Cactus Jack / Epic. Drops B4Pink EP, then debut album Pink Heartz
  • 2023
    Returns with GO+ — 12 tracks, features from Yung Kayo and DD Osama
  • 2025
    Drops Mania — 19-track statement. His most ambitious chapter yet

Do Not Sell My Personal Info

Your rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and how to exercise them.

Effective: January 1, 2025 · Last Updated: May 2025

For California Residents

The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants California residents specific rights regarding their personal information, including the right to know what data is collected, the right to delete that data, and the right to opt out of the "sale" of personal information as defined under CCPA.

If you are a California resident, this page explains what personal information this site may collect, whether it is sold, and how you can exercise your rights.

Do We Sell Your Personal Information?

No. This site does not sell your personal information. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties for monetary consideration.

This site is informational in nature. The limited data we collect — such as anonymous analytics about page visits — is used solely to improve the site and is not sold or shared for commercial purposes. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Your CCPA Rights

As a California resident, you have the following rights under CCPA:

  • Right to Know: You can request details about what personal information we collect about you and how it is used.
  • Right to Delete: You can request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt Out: You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. As noted above, we do not sell personal information, so no opt-out is currently required.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

Submitting a Request

Even though we do not sell personal information, you may still submit a request to know or delete your data. To submit a verifiable consumer request, please contact us through SoFaygo's official social media channels with the subject line "CCPA Request."

We will respond to verifiable requests within 45 days. If we require more time (up to 90 days total), we will inform you of the reason and extension in writing. There is no charge for making a request.

Opt-Out Confirmation
Because this site does not sell personal information, no formal opt-out action is required. Your privacy is already protected by default.
Note for California residents: If you believe a third-party service embedded on this site (such as Spotify or YouTube) may be collecting and sharing your data, please review their respective privacy policies and exercise your rights directly through those platforms.

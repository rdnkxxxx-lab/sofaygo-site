Accessibility

Our commitment to making this site accessible to everyone, regardless of ability or technology.

Statement Updated: May 2025

Our Commitment

We are committed to ensuring that this website is accessible to all visitors, including those with disabilities. We work to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards, and continually review and improve our design and code to remove barriers.

Accessibility is not a checkbox — it is an ongoing practice. We welcome feedback from users who encounter barriers so we can address them promptly.

What We've Done

⌨
Keyboard Navigation
All interactive elements — navigation links, buttons, accordion toggles, and media controls — are reachable and operable with a keyboard alone.
♿
Screen Reader Support
We use semantic HTML, ARIA labels, and meaningful link text so screen readers can accurately describe page content and controls to users.
◑
Color & Contrast
Text and interactive elements maintain a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 against their backgrounds, meeting WCAG AA standards for readability.
📱
Responsive Design
The site adapts to all screen sizes and supports pinch-to-zoom, ensuring usability on mobile devices and for users who need to enlarge text.
🖼
Image Descriptions
All meaningful images include descriptive alt text. Decorative images are marked appropriately so they are not announced to screen reader users.
🎬
Motion & Animation
Animations are subtle and purposeful. Users who prefer reduced motion can use their OS settings to minimize animation effects on supported browsers.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some third-party embedded content — including the Spotify player and YouTube videos — is served by external platforms and may have limitations outside our direct control. We encourage users who require accessible alternatives to visit those platforms directly, where native accessibility features are available.

Standards & Guidelines

This site aims to conform to the following standards:

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA — Web Content Accessibility Guidelines published by the W3C.
  • ARIA 1.1 — Accessible Rich Internet Applications specification for interactive components.
  • Section 508 — U.S. federal accessibility standards for electronic and information technology.

Feedback & Contact

If you experience any accessibility barriers on this site, or if you need content in an alternative format, please let us know. You can reach out through SoFaygo's official social media channels. We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days and will make reasonable accommodations as quickly as possible.

We take all accessibility concerns seriously. Your feedback directly informs our ongoing improvement efforts.

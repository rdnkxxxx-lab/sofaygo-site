Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit this site.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 · Last Updated: May 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the SoFaygo official website ("Site," "we," "us") collects, uses, and shares information about visitors. By using this Site, you agree to the practices described below. This policy applies only to this Site and does not cover third-party platforms linked from it, including Spotify, Apple Music, Ticketmaster, or the official merchandise store.

Information We Collect

This Site is primarily informational and does not require account creation or collect personal information directly. However, the following types of data may be gathered automatically or through third-party services embedded on the Site:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, device type, and referring URL — collected anonymously through standard web analytics.
  • Cookies & local storage: Small files used to remember preferences (such as theme settings) across sessions. No login or personal identification is stored.
  • Third-party embeds: The Spotify embedded player may collect data according to Spotify's Privacy Policy. YouTube embeds are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

Any data collected is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it.
  • Ensure technical functionality, security, and performance.
  • Diagnose errors or accessibility issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

This Site may use session cookies and local storage to provide a consistent experience. These include:

  • Functional cookies: Store your preferences (e.g., whether you've dismissed the intro animation) for the duration of your session.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous, aggregated data about Site traffic. No personally identifiable information is linked to analytics data.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that some Site features may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

Third-Party Links

This Site contains links to external platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, Ticketmaster, YouTube, and imsofaygo.com. Once you leave this Site, your data is governed by those platforms' own privacy policies. We encourage you to read their policies before sharing personal information with them.

Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through this Site, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale. Because this Site does not sell personal information, no opt-out is currently required. For more details, see our Do Not Sell My Personal Info page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
